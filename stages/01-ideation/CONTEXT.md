# Stage 01 - Ideation

**Purpose:** Decide which glazes to layer
**Output:** `output/combo.md`

---

## Questions to Answer

1. Which two (or three) glazes?
2. Which is base (first dip)?
3. Why this combo? (color goal, surface goal, curiosity)

---

## Ideation Sources

| Source | What It Gives |
|--------|---------------|
| `../ceramics-foundation/taxonomies/glazes.md` | All 32 studio glazes |
| Previous successful combos | What worked before |
| Contrast seeking | Gloss over matt, light over dark |
| Chemistry hunches | Copper glaze + tin glaze = ? |

---

## Combo Selection Heuristics

**High probability of interesting results:**
- Transparent over opaque (shows through)
- Light over dark (contrast at edges)
- Shino over anything (carbon trap variation)
- Matt over gloss (texture contrast)

**Lower probability:**
- Dark over dark (muddy)
- Same color family (no contrast)

---

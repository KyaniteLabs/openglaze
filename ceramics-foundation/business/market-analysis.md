## Content Strategy

### 2026 Benchmarks

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Posting frequency | Inconsistent | 3-5x/week |
| Video completion | Unknown | 70%+ |
| DM response time | Unknown | <4 hours |
| Hashtag count | 27-30 | 3-5 |
| Caption length | 227 avg | 300-500 |
| Questions in posts | 10% | 30-50% |

### Algorithm Signals Priority

| Signal | Focus Area | Target | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|--------|
| Watch time | Yes | 70%+ completion rate | Hook in first 2 seconds, keep videos 15-30s |
| Sends per reach | Yes | DM shares = #1 way to reach non-followers | Add 'Send to a friend who...' CTAs |
| Saves | Yes | Educational content gets saved | Include tips, techniques, 'save this for later' |

### Content Mix Recommendation

| Format | Percentage | Purpose |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Reels (process) | 50% | Reach new audiences |
| Reels (finished pieces) | 20% | Showcase work |
| Carousel (educational) | 20% | Get saves |
| Stories | Daily | Nurture existing followers |

### Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Time | Content Type |
|-----|------|--------------|
| Monday | 8:00 AM | Reel (process/transformation) |
| Wednesday | 5:00 PM | Carousel or Reel (finished piece) |
| Friday | 8:00 AM | Reel (behind-the-scenes) |
